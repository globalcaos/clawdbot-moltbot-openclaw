## Planned Expansion

### Vertical East-West Panels

**Concept:** Additional panels mounted vertically, facing east and west.

**Benefits:**
- Captures morning and evening sun
- Complements midday production from roof panels
- May smooth out production curve
- Potentially better for winter sun angles

**Challenges:**
- Structural mounting
- Wind load
- Aesthetics
- Permit requirements
