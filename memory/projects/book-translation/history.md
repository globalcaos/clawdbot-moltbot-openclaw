## Style Guide

### Vocabulary
- Simple, age-appropriate (9-year-old comprehension)
- Avoid complex sentence structures
- Preserve wonder and mystery

### Punctuation
- Em-dash (—) for dialogue, not quotation marks
- Spanish dialogue conventions adapted to English

### Tone
- Preserve suspense and wonder
- Maintain warmth of family relationships
- Keep spiritual elements accessible to children

### Format
- HTML output with specific CSS classes
- Chapter divisions preserved
- Illustrations placeholders noted
